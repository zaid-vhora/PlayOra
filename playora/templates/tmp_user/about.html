🌟 Our Story

Born from a Parent's Frustration

In 2026, our founder zaid vhora spent ₹8,000 on a LEGO Technic set for his 7-year-old son. Three weeks later, it sat in a corner, forgotten. That moment sparked an idea — what if every family could access premium toys without the financial burden?

ToyRent was born to make playtime magical, sustainable, and affordable for every Indian family. Today, we serve 50,000+ families across 25+ cities with a library of 5,000+ toys.
